🔴 Critical (direct SEO + accessibility impact)

1 · All <html lang="en-US"> on Thai pages

Pages: ALL 9 /th/ pages.

Impact: Google ranks pages as English content — Thai-language search visibility drops. Screen readers pronounce Thai text with English phonetics → accessibility fail.

Fix: Change to lang="th" on every /th/ template.

2 · og:locale = en_US on Thai pages

Pages: ALL 9 /th/ pages.

Impact: Facebook / LINE / Twitter shares treat the content as English. Wrong locale-targeted preview rendering, wrong canonical clusters.

Fix: Set <meta property="og:locale" content="th_TH">.

3 · 6 of 9 pages have ENGLISH <title>

Pages: Home, Brands, Yokohama, Ogura Clutch, Applications, Services.

Examples:

"Home - RIK Industries Pte Ltd - Thailand"
"Brands - RIK Industries Pte Ltd - Thailand"
"Services - RIK Industries Pte Ltd - Thailand"

Impact: Thai-language Google users searching "คลัตช์อุตสาหกรรม" or "เบรกอุตสาหกรรม" won't see these in results. Lost top-of-funnel traffic.

Note: 3 pages (cement-mining, marine, printing) DO have Thai titles — content team CAN do this. Just apply consistently.

4 · No hreflang alternate links

Pages: ALL 9 /th/ pages.

Impact: Google doesn't know that /th/applications/marine/ is the Thai version of /en/applications/marine/ → duplicate-content risk + lost link equity between language variants.

Fix:

<link rel="alternate" hreflang="en" href="https://rikind.com/en/applications/marine/" />
<link rel="alternate" hreflang="th" href="https://rikind.com/th/applications/marine/" />
<link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default" href="https://rikind.com/" />
5 · 3 pages have NO <h1> tag

Pages: /th/brands/ · /th/applications/ · /th/services/ (all "index" pages).

Impact: No primary heading = critical accessibility + SEO miss. Search engines and screen readers can't identify the page's main topic.

6 · 2 pages missing og:image

Pages: /th/applications/ · /th/services/.

Impact: Social shares show no preview image — sharply lower CTR on Facebook / LINE.

7 · Brands index meta description is only 10 chars

Value: "แบรนด์เด่น" (10 chars total).

Impact: Google won't use this as the SERP snippet — auto-generates from page content instead, losing CTR control.

Fix: Expand to 140-160 chars with brand names and value props.

💡 Root cause

The site appears to be WordPress multisite where the Thai sub-site shares a template hard-coded with English locale metadata. The page content is in Thai (rendered from Thai post records), but <html lang>, og:locale, and several auto-generated titles come from the theme/template layer which assumes English.

ภาษา = ปัจจุบันมี 2 source-of-truth ที่ขัดกัน:
  Page CONTENT: ภาษาไทย ✅
  Page METADATA: ภาษาอังกฤษ ❌
→ WordPress + Polylang/WPML plugin มักจัดการนี้ผ่าน hooks. แก้ที่ theme ครั้งเดียว = แก้ทั้งหมด
