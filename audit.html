All H1s contain a 4-tab gap between segments — e.g. "KUNNAPAB HOME SOLUTION\t\t\t\tคุณภาพ โฮมโซลูชั่น".

Likely cause: WordPress theme template uses <br> or block-layout markup with literal tabs instead of clean structure.

Fix: Find & remove \t in the theme PHP / Gutenberg blocks. Or replace tabs with a <br>.

About pages typically need team photos, brand-story imagery, or proof-of-work for trust. Currently text-only.

Suggested: Add 2–3 images — team photo, showroom, sample installation.
